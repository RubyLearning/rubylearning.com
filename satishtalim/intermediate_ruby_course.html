What's an Intermediate Ruby Course?

This Intermediate Ruby Course course will bridge the gap from learning basic or core Ruby, by providing what it takes to use some of the more advanced Ruby Libraries (Core, standard, and additional) and providing the technical skill to apply Ruby in our soon to be announced Advanced Ruby Course. The Advanced Ruby course will cover such topics as: meta-programming, design patterns and best practices (OOD, TDD, BDD).
